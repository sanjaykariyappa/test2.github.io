About Me

I am a Sr. Research Scientist at Nvidia. My research is focused on Trustworthy AI, with a particular emphasis on security and privacy.

I obtained my PhD from Georgia Tech in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Moinuddin K. Qureshi. In addition to my work on Trustworthy AI, I have published in the areas of computer architecture, hardware security and AI accelerators.

I previously worked at JP Morgan’s Explainable AI CoE. I’ve also been fortunate to intern at several industrial research labs including FAIR and IBM Research during my PhD.
